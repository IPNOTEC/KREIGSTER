Choosing the Right Fundraising Tools

Strategic capital acquisition for growth
💰

Immediate Funds Strategy

Focus: Maximum capital infusion

  • Convertible Notes / SAFE
  • Revenue-Based Financing
  • Quick closing (4-6 weeks)
  • Higher dilution accepted
👥

Expertise Strategy

Focus: Strategic mentorship & networks

  • Strategic Angel Investors
  • Fashion Accelerators
  • Longer due diligence
  • Board participation
⚡

Speed Strategy

Focus: Rapid execution & scaling

  • Micro VCs with quick decisions
  • Founder-friendly terms
  • Minimal reporting overhead
  • Focus on growth metrics

Ideal Strategy: Balanced Approach

Why this approach: Start with SAFE/Convertible Notes from angel + micro VC for quick capital, while bringing 1-2 strategic angels for mentorship. This provides immediate funds (₹15-20L) while building the right advisory network.

Timeline: Use traction to unlock follow-on equity round within 12-18 months at improved valuation.

Target Mix: 70% immediate capital, 20% expertise, 10% speed-focused investors

Future Innovations: Building a Digital Fashion Hub

Pioneering the future of sustainable fashion tech

Digital Fashion Hub

A Digital App connecting digital designers, manufacturing, and customers with personalized recommendations based on:

  • Brand preferences
  • Color & print preferences
  • Fit & sizing data
  • Regional specifications

Revenue Model: Revenue sharing between designers, manufacturers, and platform

Cloud Tailoring & Smart Mirror

Complete customization module with:

  • Virtual try-on technology
  • AI-powered fit recommendations
  • Real-time customization options
  • Smart mirror integration for virtual styling

Cactui Leather Initiative

Responding to growing demand for conscious alternatives to animal leather:

  • Build small-scale factories on Cactui farms
  • Select water-scarce regions for self-sustaining business model
  • Provide training and employment opportunities
  • Create eco-friendly leather alternative for fashion industry

Strategic Impact

These innovations position Kreigster at the forefront of sustainable fashion technology, creating new revenue streams while strengthening our core mission of ethical production and community empowerment.
